CK FBLA

One Story, One Chapter

Established 2017

FBLA also known as Future Leaders of America, is national program that brings students together to compete in region, state, and at nationals. We are proud to have FBLA at Cross Keys High School because we value our students skills and career goals. FBLA helps enhance skills in public speaking, competions, leadership, and networking. Each year we go to an annual rally convention that lets our members interact with other FBLA chapters around Georgia. This year we're also inviting interesting business owners to come and talk to our students about real world business and life skills. If you're interested in joining FBLA, please contact our advisor, Mr. Green located in room 201 or Ms. Bryant in room 314.

Bronze Chapter of the Year




With over 100 members this year, we are proud to announce that our chapter has been awarded the Bronze Chapter of the year. Special congratulations to our advisors Mr. Green and Mrs. Bryant.



This upcoming year, our goal is to work twice as hard to reach our goal of having silver chapter of the year at our annual state compeition.
